Privacy Policy

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

'Growing Semiconductors' is a game that can be played without a separate account registration. The developer does not directly collect or store any personal information on servers. All game progress data is stored solely on the user's device.

2. Categories of Personal Information Processed

The developer does not directly collect any personal information.

3. Information Collection by Third-Party Services

This app includes the following third-party services (SDKs) for the purpose of providing advertisements and app analytics. These service providers may collect and process device information, advertising IDs, and other data in order to deliver their services.

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
    Data collected: Ad data, device identifiers (Advertising ID), app usage history, crash logs
    Purpose: Serving personalized ads, ad performance analysis, fraud prevention
    View Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services (Core Services)
    Data collected: Device information, log data
    Purpose: App security, updates, and support for essential features

4. In-App Purchases

This app offers in-app purchases through the Apple App Store.

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed by the Apple App Store. The developer does not directly collect or store any payment information such as credit card details.
  • Purchase History: Purchase history is linked to your Apple account and can be restored via the 'Settings → Restore Purchases' feature in the app.
  • Refund Policy: Cancellations and refunds are subject to Apple App Store policies. You may request a refund at Apple's refund page.

5. Retention and Deletion of Data

Since the developer does not separately collect or store personal information, uninstalling the app or clearing its data will immediately delete all game progress information stored on the device.
